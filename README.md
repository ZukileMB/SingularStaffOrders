 ```JSON # SingularStaffOrders

This is an online shopping Application aiming to help internal staff members to place orders.
In this application Users are not required to Login
Users have an allowance to click in each product to view the description 
Users may select from the list of available products and add to cart.
Once added products to the cart the user have a choice to add the quantity per product 
When adding multiple products they will be listed and the price will be calculated as they add to the cart.
When done you may capture the details of the customer 
then you may proceed to place an order.
